# What is Outreach?

A large component of our club outside of making rockets is outreach! In outreach we teach the local community about rocketry, composites and other concepts in STEM with a variety of activities.

# Why do we do Outreach?

Originally the club started doing outreach to the local community as it is a requirement in the NASA Student Launch compeititon. As our team has continued to grow we have far exceeded the requirements of NASA Student Launch but still really value the experience outreach gives our members and the community! Below are some of the major reasons we continue to do outreach events.

## 1. New members

While it may seem counter intuitive, reaching out to the community is a fantastic way to get more people into rocketry and in the long term possibly in our club! Rocketry is currently fairly unknown to the general public and increasing awarness about something we are so passionate about puts it on more people's radar especially when these students eventually graduate from highschool and are looking for possible clubs to join when they go to college. 

## 2. Club experience

A fantastic way to teach new members about the club is to bring them to outreach events! Learning new information abotu rocketry can be challenging and is exactly the point of outreach. Going to these events can teach new members about how rockets work while also giving them opportunities to present. Going to outreach events allows newer members of the club to build presentation skills and the ability to talk about the engineering work they do in a low pressure enviorment. This sets up new members to perform better when they need to pull on this experience in career fairs and interviews and eventually a job!

## 3. Networking

Outreach events are a fantastic way to meet people who are interested in rockets and space! In the past outreach events have given the club networking opportunities for new outreach events, possible speakers, and potential sponsorship leads. 

## 4. They are fun

Lastly, but certaintly not least, outreach events are fun! Going to outreach events, speaking with members of the community and teaching the younger generation about STEM is a rewarding and fun experience. It is a tradition in our club to have slides that are inside jokes for people who went to the outreach events that past week. These slides only exist because of how much fun outreach events are to go to! While outreach events are challenging, mentally taxing and tiring, they are also very rewarding. After spending a few hours or a day on outreach you feel fulfilled and know you spent your time doing something valuble productive.
