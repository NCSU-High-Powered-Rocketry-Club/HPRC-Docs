# Deployment

## Single Deploy vs Dual Deploy
Single deployment uses only a main chute that is deployed at apogee, while dual deployment uses a drogue chute at apogee and a main chute at a lower altitude during descent. Electronic is required for dual deployment.

## Dual-Step Dual-Deploy
The standard deployment mechanism utilized by the club.

## Single-Step Dual-Deploy
Employs the use of a chute release (Jolly Logic) or cable cutters (Cat Claws)

## Head-End Dual-Deploy
Parachute is in nosecone

## Motor Ejection
No electronics necessary, delay grain drilling
