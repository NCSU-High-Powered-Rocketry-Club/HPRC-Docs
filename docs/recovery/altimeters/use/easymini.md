# EasyMini

The AltusMetrum EasyMini is considered one of the most reliable altimeters to use for deployment. It is commonly used in HPRC as a secondary altimeter as it is a sure-fire backup. The EasyMini has one of the smallest footprints measuring 38 x 21 mm. 

AltusMetrum is also a great brand because they provide all of their products open source including the schematic, BoM, and firmware.
