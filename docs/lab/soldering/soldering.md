# Soldering

Soldering is the process of joining two or more electronic components together by melting a [filler metal](tools_and_materials.md#solder) to create a strong electrical and mechanical bond.

This guide will cover the basics of soldering, including the tools and materials needed, the steps to soldering, some tips for achieving good results, and of course safety precautions.
